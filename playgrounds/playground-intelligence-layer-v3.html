Profile to Checklist Router
Deterministic. Same input = same output. A 68-year-old married Californian always gets the same 12 documents. No AI needed — this is pure rules, and that's a strength.
Trace a scenario
Margaret gets divorced and moves from California to Florida
1
Trigger: Profile update — CA to FL, married to divorced
2
Re-route: FL is not community property. Remove spousal consent rules.
3
+6 documents: FL Lady Bird Deed, QDRO, Updated Beneficiaries, FL Healthcare Surrogate
4
Score impact: 62% drops to 38%. Life event = new requirements = fresh urgency.
Why this matters
Divorce + relocation is a common trigger for the 60+ demographic. This scenario shows the product adapting in real-time — exactly when the family needs it most.

Document Auto-Classification
Upload a PDF. The system reads it, identifies the document type, assigns searchable tags, and closes the gap on the checklist — automatically. This is the right amount of AI for this product.

The rules engine is the moat. The AI just makes uploading smooth. Don't over-invest in AI when the knowledge base is what clients can't replicate.
